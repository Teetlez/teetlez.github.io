Procedural Terrain


by Timothy Clocksin

Discription

A non-interactive terrain generator designed to create: trees, grass, mountians, snow, beaches, lakes/ponds, clouds, and hills. The generation ulilizes perlin noise to create natural looking hills and clouds with a isometric viewpoint.

For more information on the function of Perlin Noise, visit:

en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perlin_noise

Controls

  • None: just sit back, relax, and enjoy the view.
