Ray-casting


by Timothy Clocksin

Discription

An attempt to recreate the raycast method used in early 3D videogames such as DOOM and Wolfenstein 3D. The method utilizes shooting a ray through a 2D array for every vertical row of pixels. The height of the darkened line of pixels or "wall" is determined by the length of the ray.

For a more in-depth idea of what is going on, check out Lorde Vandevenne's website where he gives a better technical explenation on the technique:

lodev.org/cgtutor/raycasting.html

Controls

  • UP - Move forwards
  • DOWN - Move backwards
  • LEFT - Turn counter-clockwise
  • RIGHT - Turn clockwise
